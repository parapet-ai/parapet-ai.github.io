An offline AI grading platform for secondary education. Key Innovation: 7B Training on 6GB VRAM

Everyone says you need 10–12 GB VRAM to fine-tune a 7B model. We proved you can do it with 6 GB — on a 6-year-old consumer laptop.

91.7% tool-calling accuracy on a 3B model. 43 models catalogued. 0.008 kg CO2 per training run. 70W power cap. 2 minutes to install — zero internet forever after.

Sustainability by Design

Dark mode only — saves 40–60% on OLED. 70W GPU power cap extends hardware lifespan. CO₂ tracked on every training run. Runs on refurbished consumer hardware. No data centers. No cooling overhead. No server farms. Aligned with UN SDGs 4 (Quality Education), 9 (Innovation), 12 (Responsible Consumption), 13 (Climate Action), and 16 (Strong Institutions).
